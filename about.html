What is COD'BLAST?

      COD'BLAST is a game inspired by Block Blast, made in JavaScript and HTML5 Canvas in 2026 for our college JavaScript module. Place your blocks wisely on the grid to score points! The game ends when none of the player's available blocks can be placed.

      Is it free?

      Yes, COD'BLAST is completely free to play and open source, with no ads.

      Who is behind COD'BLAST?

      COD'BLAST was created by a team of students from a Computer Science college. Most of the game logic was developed by Esteban, with Maxime behind most of the Views part of the MVC, while Emery made the website's UI.
